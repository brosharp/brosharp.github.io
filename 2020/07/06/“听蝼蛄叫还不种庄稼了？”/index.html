“听蝼蛄叫还不种庄稼了？”

There were many mole crickets and we could hear them chirp at night. There was a popular expression among us: “Don’t stop planting wheat just because mole crickets chirp,” which means you should not let little problems or risks prevent you from doing what is necessary. I still follow such wisdom today. Weijian Shan Out of the Gobi

去年初秋翻到这一段时心中忽有所悟，暗下决心要完成一桩拖了很久的事情，到本月初终于告成。聊记一笔。
